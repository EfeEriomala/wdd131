Delta State, Nigeria

Official flag of Delta State

Delta State is one of the 36 states of Nigeria, located in the South-South geopolitical zone. It is known for its oil and gas resources, which significantly contribute to the Nigerian economy. The state's capital is Asaba. Delta State is home to diverse ethnic groups, including the Urhobo, Ijaw, Igbo, and Itsekiri. The state experiences a tropical climate, characterized by high temperatures and humidity. It also has a rich cultural heritage, with various traditional festivals and arts.
